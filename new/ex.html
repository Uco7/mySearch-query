UNN Admission Guide

  1. Visit the UNN admission portal at www.unn.edu.ng
  2. Click on the "Admissions" tab and select "Apply Now"
  3. Create an account or log in if you already have one
  4. Fill out the online application form and upload required documents
  5. Pay the application fee online
  6. Submit the form and print your application slip

Below are the general admission requirements for UNN:

  • Minimum of five O’Level credits including English and Mathematics
  • JAMB UTME score meets the departmental cut-off mark
  • Successful completion of post-UTME screening
  • Birth certificate or age declaration
  • Original and photocopies of credentials

The admission deadline for UNN varies each year. Generally, applications close:

  • First batch: Around August
  • Second batch: Around October
  • Supplementary admissions: Early December

Visit the UNN website for the latest updates.

  1. Go to the UNN admission status portal: www.unn.edu.ng
  2. Click on “Check Admission Status”
  3. Enter your JAMB registration number
  4. Click on the “Check Status” button
  5. If admitted, follow the instructions to proceed with acceptance
